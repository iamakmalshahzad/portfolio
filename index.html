About

Infusing businesses with the magic of pixels and code, I craft online success one line at a time.

Hello! My name is Akmal Shahzad and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2016 when I tried to edit a custom WordPress theme — Little did I know that this simple endeavor would become my gateway to Web Development, driving me to explore it's limitless possibilities.

Over the past 6 years, I've been on an incredible adventure as a freelance web developer. During this time, I've had the opportunity to work remotely with agencies, offer my insights to startups, and collaborate with talented individuals to create captivating digital experiences for businesses and users alike. I possess a quiet confidence, an insatiable curiosity, and a perpetual drive to enhance my skills by taking on one coding challenge at a time.
